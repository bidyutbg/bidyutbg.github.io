Research Interests

My research focuses on understanding atmospheric dynamics and variability, and their response to warming, with an emphasis on the role of atmospheric convection, its organization, and the subsequent dynamic interactions. To this end, I perform experiments using a hierarchy of models. In my research, the Indian summer monsoon is an elemental component.
