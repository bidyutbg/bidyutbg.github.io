Research Interests

The Indian summer monsoon is a central component of my research, which focuses on understanding atmospheric dynamics and variability, and their response to warming, with particular emphasis on atmospheric convection, its organisation, and the resulting dynamical interactions. To address these, I use a hierarchy of models.
