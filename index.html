Bidyut Bikash Goswami, PhD

Atmospheric Scientist | Climate Modeler | Educator

Researcher at Institute of Science and Technology Austria (ISTA) and Post-doc at the Muller Group.

I study the dynamics of tropical convection, land-ocean heterogeneity, and climate variability using models such as CESM, SAM, and CFS, combined with large-scale datasets like ERA5 and TRMM. I’ve conducted research at ISTA, and collaborated with scientists across institutions including the University of Alaska Fairbanks.

I've taught graduate courses, mentored doctoral researchers, and conducted short courses on Python at EGU. I also have experience with high-performance computing systems such as Prithvi, Nestor, Cedar, Dalma, and ISTA-HPC.

For collaborations or questions, please email me.
